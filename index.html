Your Questions, Answered

Clear info so you feel confident booking.

What services can I book with Sadaf Bridal? +
You can book bridal makeup, occasional/formal makeup, hairstyling (blow-dry, curls, upstyles), facials (deep cleansing, glow/anti-aging) and eyebrow shaping. Packages can be customised for your event.
Is a facial included before every makeup appointment? +
Yes. A facial/skin-prep session is done before every makeup appointment to help create a smoother base, better blend, and longer-lasting finish.
How do I Book an Appointment (and how far ahead should I book)? +
Simply click Book an Appointment and choose your service and preferred time. For bridal bookings, it’s best to book as early as possible to secure your date (especially during peak wedding season).
Which products do you use for makeup and facials? +
I use professional-grade products selected for your skin type and desired finish. If you have sensitivities or preferences, let me know during booking and I’ll tailor the products accordingly.
Do you offer bridal trials before the wedding day? +
Yes. Bridal trials are available and recommended, especially if you want to test a specific look or you’re unsure between styles. We’ll refine the makeup and hair so you feel 100% confident on the day.
What are your booking, reschedule, and cancellation terms? +
Appointments can be rescheduled with notice, subject to availability. For cancellations, any deposit/booking fee is handled according to the service type and time of cancellation. Please refer to the booking confirmation for the exact terms, or message me if you need help.
